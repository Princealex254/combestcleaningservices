Yes! We bring all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment. We use professional-grade, eco-friendly products that are safe for your family and pets. You don't need to provide anything - we come fully equipped.
Do you offer one-time or recurring cleaning? +
We offer both! Whether you need a one-time deep clean, weekly maintenance, or monthly service, we can customize a cleaning schedule that works for your needs and budget. Contact us to discuss your preferred frequency.
Are your pest control methods safe for pets and children? +
Absolutely! We use pet and child-safe pest control methods and products. Our team is trained in the latest safe pest control techniques. We'll provide detailed information about any treatments and safety precautions before we begin.

We serve Nairobi and surrounding areas including Westlands, Karen, Kiambu, Thika, and Kisumu. We're expanding our reach across Kenya. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.
